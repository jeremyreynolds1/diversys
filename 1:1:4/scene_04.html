Preventing Workplace Harassment

Course Objectives

Course Objectives
This course is intended to cover the following:

  • The definition of unlawful sexual harassment.
  • Examples of harassment
  • How to protect yourself against harassment
  • Steps to take if you believe you are being harassed
  • What to do if you witness harassment
  • "What to do if an employee approaches you with a complain of harassment or discrimination"
  • How to implement harassment policies and their essential elements
  • How to respond to a complaint and the company's obligation to conduct an investigation
